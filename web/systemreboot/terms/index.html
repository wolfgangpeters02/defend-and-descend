2. Description of Service

System: Reboot is a tower defense game for iOS in which you defend a virtual computer system against cyber threats. The App allows you to:

  • Place and upgrade firewalls (towers) to defend your motherboard against viruses
  • Fight boss encounters with unlockable protocols (weapons)
  • Unlock and manage sectors on your motherboard
  • Upgrade system components (CPU, PSU, RAM, HDD, Network)
  • Earn and spend in-game currency (Hash) through gameplay
  • Collect blueprints and compile new protocols

The App is designed for personal, non-commercial use only.
