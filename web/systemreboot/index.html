What is System: Reboot?

System: Reboot is a tower defense game set inside a computer. Viruses are attacking your motherboard, and your job is to stop them before they reach the CPU. Place firewalls to defend lanes, upgrade your system components, and fight bosses that are immune to tower damage.

The game blends classic tower defense with idle mechanics and boss encounters. When you're actively playing, you manage tower placement, fight bosses, and optimize your defenses. When you're away, the game simulates your setup and rewards you with offline earnings when you return.

How does it work?

Your motherboard is divided into sectors, each with lanes where viruses spawn. You place firewalls along these lanes to intercept and destroy threats. Each firewall costs Hash (the in-game currency) and uses power from your PSU.

Periodically, bosses appear. These are powerful threats immune to firewall damage – you fight them directly using protocols (weapons). Defeating bosses earns Hash rewards and can drop blueprints that unlock new protocols with unique abilities.

Between sessions, you upgrade system components: a faster CPU multiplies your income, more PSU power lets you deploy more towers, additional RAM speeds up gameplay, and Network upgrades multiply your Hash earnings.

The Efficiency System

Unlike traditional tower defense games where leaks mean game over, System: Reboot uses an efficiency system. When viruses leak through your defenses, your system efficiency drops, reducing your Hash income. This means weaker defenses don't end your game – they slow your progress.

Keep your firewalls strong and well-placed to maintain high efficiency. The better your defense, the more you earn – both while playing and while away.

Game Features

Tower Defense

Place firewalls across multiple sectors. Each tower has range, damage, and fire rate. Strategize placement to maximize coverage.

Boss Encounters

Fight the Cyberboss, Trojan Wyrm, Void Harbinger, and Overclocker. Each boss has unique attack patterns and mechanics.

Protocol Arsenal

Collect blueprints from boss kills. Compile and upgrade protocols with unique stats. Equip them for boss encounters.

Component Upgrades

Upgrade CPU, PSU, RAM, HDD, and Network. Each component affects different aspects of your game performance.

Sector Expansion

Start with one sector and unlock more as you progress. More sectors mean more lanes, more threats, and more earning potential.

Idle Economy

Your defenses run while you're away. Come back to collect offline earnings based on your setup's efficiency.

Privacy and Data

System: Reboot requires no account and collects no personal data. The only data that leaves your device is anonymous gameplay analytics (via Mixpanel) to help us balance the game. Your game progress is stored locally on your device.

There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no tracking. Details are in our Privacy Policy.
